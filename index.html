Campaign Overview

The Vigilance Awareness Campaign 2024 in Kathara Area focused on promoting integrity, social responsibility, environmental conservation, health, and stakeholder engagement. The campaign successfully fostered a culture of ethical conduct and transparency through diverse activities involving employees, students, and the community.

Testimonial

The Vigilance Awareness Campaign 2024 in Kathara Area was a resounding success, marked by active participation from employees, students, and the public. Through educational activities, mass outreach programs, and competitions, the campaign successfully promoted the values of integrity, accountability, and transparency. The involvement of youth through school and college programs ensured that the message of vigilance reached the next generation, fostering a culture of ethical conduct for the future.
